About MMUST

Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology (MMUST) is a leading institution of higher learning located in Kakamega, Kenya.Established in 2007, MMUST is named after the renowned Kenyan politician and freedom fighter, Hon. Masinde Muliro. Committed to fostering excellence in science and technology education, the university has a strong emphasis on research, innovation, and community engagement. Beyond academics, MMUST encourages extracurricular activities, fostering holistic development and a sense of social responsibility. We are deeply rooted in our commitment to advancing scientific knowledge, technological innovation, and contributing to the growth and development of our local and global communities. Join us on our journey of knowledge and discovery at Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology.

Our institutionboasts a diverse and vibrant academic community, comprising talented faculty and enthusiastic students from various backgrounds. At MMUST, we are dedicated to providing quality education, empowering students with knowledge, skills, and values to thrive in a rapidly changing world.Our state-of-the-art facilities, modern laboratories, and comprehensive library resources contribute to a conducive learning environment.
